### Pricing Transparency

**How Triple Pricing Works**

We believe healthy food should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their financial situation. That's why we created our triple pricing systemâ€”a dignified way to ensure universal access while maintaining sustainability.

**Community Tier**: Covers the cost of ingredients and basic operations. Choose this tier if money is tight or you're going through a difficult time. No proof required, no questions asked.

**Fair Tier**: Covers the full cost of the meal including ingredients, labor, rent, and operations. This is our suggested price and helps us maintain quality and sustainability.

**Supporter Tier**: Pays a bit extra to help subsidize community tier meals for others. If you're in a position to help, this is a beautiful way to support your neighbors.

The beauty of this system is that it balances out naturally. Some pay less, some pay more, and everyone eats well. It's community economics at its finest.

### FAQ Section

**What is Satvic food?**

Satvic food is based on ancient Ayurvedic principles and consists of pure, wholesome, plant-based ingredients that promote clarity, peace, and vitality. It excludes onion, garlic, and other tamasic (heavy) foods, focusing instead on fresh vegetables, fruits, nuts, seeds, whole grains, and dairy alternatives.

**How does the triple pricing work?**

Simply choose the tier that feels right for you when ordering. Community tier if you're on a budget, Fair tier to cover our costs, or Supporter tier to help others. There's no verification processâ€”we trust you to choose honestly based on your circumstances.

**Do I need to be vegetarian to join?**

Not at all! While our food is 100% plant-based, we welcome everyone regardless of their dietary choices. Many of our members are on a journey toward plant-based eating, and we're here to support wherever you are on that path.

**Can I volunteer even if I have no special skills?**

**Is there a membership fee?**
