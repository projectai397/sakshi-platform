**Date:** November 8, 2025
**Author:** Manus AI

## 1. Executive Summary

This document proposes a groundbreaking new feature for the Sakshi platform: **SakshiChain**. This feature integrates a **Digital Product Passport (DPP)** system with a **tokenized rewards economy**, leveraging blockchain technology to enhance trust, transparency, and user engagement. SakshiChain will create a unique, verifiable history for every item on the platform, transforming them into digital assets and rewarding users for their participation in the circular economy. This will not only solve core problems in the resale market but also establish Sakshi as a pioneer in the future of sustainable e-commerce.

## 2. The Opportunity: Beyond Resale

The current resale market, while growing rapidly, faces significant challenges related to **authenticity, transparency, and user incentives** [2]. Our research into emerging trends reveals a convergence of three key areas that present a unique opportunity for Sakshi:

1.  **The Rise of Digital Product Passports:** Driven by consumer demand for transparency and upcoming EU regulations (ESPR), DPPs are set to become the standard for product traceability [1].
2.  **The Power of Web3 & Tokenization:** Decentralized technologies offer new models for ownership, community governance, and economic incentives that are perfectly suited for circular economies [3].
3.  **The Evolution of Recommerce:** Leading platforms are moving beyond simple listings to offer value-added services like RaaS (Resale-as-a-Service) and sophisticated AI-powered tools [2].

By integrating these trends, Sakshi can create a powerful new value proposition that goes far beyond what current competitors offer.

## 3. Feature Proposal: SakshiChain

SakshiChain consists of two interconnected components:

### 3.1. Digital Product Passports (DPPs)

Every item listed on Sakshi will be minted as a unique Non-Fungible Token (NFT) on a scalable, low-cost blockchain. This NFT will serve as the item's Digital Product Passport, containing a rich, immutable record of its entire lifecycle.

**DPP Data Points:**

| Data Category | Information Stored |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Provenance** | Original brand, material composition, manufacturing date. |
| **Ownership History** | A secure, anonymous record of all previous owners. |
| **Transaction History** | All sales, prices, and dates within the Sakshi ecosystem. |
| **Care & Repair Log** | Records of repairs, alterations, or upcycling activities. |
| **Impact Score** | A dynamically calculated score showing the item's positive environmental impact. |

This system provides an unparalleled level of trust and transparency, effectively eliminating counterfeit concerns and allowing buyers to understand the full story of their purchase.

### 3.2. Tokenized Rewards Economy (SakshiCoin - SAK)

We will introduce a new utility token, **SakshiCoin (SAK)**, to power the platform's economy and incentivize positive user behavior. SAK will be earned by users for actions that contribute to the circular ecosystem.

**Earning SakshiCoin (SAK):**

- **Listing Quality Items:** Earn SAK for providing detailed, accurate product information.
- **Making Purchases:** Receive SAK as a cashback reward.
- **Repairing & Upcycling:** Earn significant SAK for extending an item's life.
- **Community Engagement:** Get rewarded for writing helpful reviews, participating in forums, and referring new users.

**Using SakshiCoin (SAK):**

- **Purchase Discounts:** Apply SAK to get discounts on any item.
- **Platform Governance:** Use SAK to vote on community proposals, such as which charities to support or which new features to build.
- **Exclusive Access:** Stake SAK to get early access to new listings or limited-edition drops.
- **Service Payments:** Pay for services like professional item authentication or repair services within the platform.

This tokenized system transforms users from passive participants into active stakeholders in the platform's success.
