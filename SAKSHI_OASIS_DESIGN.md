# Sakshi Oasis - India's First Technology Disconnection Sanctuary

## Executive Summary

**Sakshi Oasis** is India's first wellness sanctuary designed to help people **disconnect from technology and reconnect with mind, body, and spirit**. Inspired by Museum of the Future's Al Waha in Dubai, but deeply rooted in **Indian healing traditions** (Ayurveda, Yoga, Vedic wisdom), Sakshi Oasis combines ancient practices with cutting-edge technology to create transformative experiences.

Unlike Al Waha's 5 therapies, Sakshi Oasis offers **12 unique experiences** across 5,000+ sq ft, with **triple pricing** for universal access, **AI-personalized journeys**, and **complete biometric tracking** for measurable outcomes.

## 12 Transformative Experiences

### 1. PRANAM (Welcome) - Olfactory & Sound Reset

**Inspired by**: Al Waha's tribulus omanense fountain

**Indian Adaptation**:
- **Scent**: Sandalwood, jasmine, tulsi (holy basil), rose
- **Sound**: Temple bells, conch shell (shankh), singing bowls
- **Visual**: Diya (oil lamp) lighting ceremony
- **Technology**: Scent diffusion system, spatial audio

**Experience**: Visitors enter through an archway with cascading water feature infused with sacred Indian flowers. Temple bells chime softly. A digital diya lights up as they approach, symbolizing inner light awakening.

**Duration**: 2-3 minutes

**Purpose**: Mental palate cleanser, transition from outer world to inner sanctuary

---

### 2. CHARAN SPARSH (Sacred Walking) - Movement Meditation

**Inspired by**: Al Waha's sand bath

**Indian Adaptation**:
- **Visual**: Projection of Ganga river, lotus petals, rangoli patterns
- **Surface**: Soft textured floor mimicking temple stone, beach sand, forest floor
- **Audio**: River sounds, bird songs, rustling leaves
- **Technology**: Real-time projection mapping, pressure sensors

**Experience**: Walk barefoot across an interactive floor that responds to each step. Footprints create ripples in projected water, lotus petals bloom, rangoli patterns emerge. Encourages slow, mindful walking (like parikrama around temples).

**Duration**: 5-10 minutes

**Purpose**: Grounding, mindfulness, connection to earth

### 3. SPARSH CHIKITSA (Touch Therapy) - Ultrasonic Healing

**Inspired by**: Al Waha's ultrasonic therapy

**Indian Adaptation**:
- **Mudra Integration**: Hands placed in specific mudras (Gyan, Prana, Apana)
- **Marma Points**: Vibrations target Ayurvedic marma points on hands
- **Copper Plates**: Engraved with sacred geometry (Sri Yantra, Om)
- **Technology**: Ultrasonic haptic + heat therapy

**Experience**: Sit in padmasana or sukhasana, place hands over copper plates engraved with Sri Yantra. Feel gentle vibrations and warmth targeting marma points. Guided audio explains mudra benefits.

**Duration**: 10-15 minutes

**Purpose**: Restore hand sensitivity, activate marma points, balance doshas

### 4. PRITHVI SANTULAN (Earth Grounding) - Frequency Therapy

**Inspired by**: Al Waha's grounding therapy

- **Barefoot Standing**: On copper/stone platform
- **Technology**: Sub-frequency generators, singing bowl automation

**Experience**: Stand barefoot between two large singing bowls on a copper platform. Invisible frequencies (7.83 Hz + chakra frequencies) emanate from bowls. Feel vibrations through feet, up spine, to crown.

**Duration**: 5-10 minutes

**Purpose**: Grounding, EMF protection, chakra balancing
