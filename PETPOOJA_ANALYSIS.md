## PetPooja Features Summary

Based on research, PetPooja is a comprehensive restaurant management platform with the following core capabilities:

### 1. Core POS Features
- **3-Click Billing**: Quick order taking and bill generation
- **KOT Management**: Kitchen Order Ticket printing and tracking
- **Table Management**: Visual table layout, table merging, bill splitting
- **Multi-Counter Support**: Multiple billing counters synced to master station
- **Captain App**: Mobile app for waiters to take orders tableside

### 2. Inventory Management
- Real-time stock tracking
- Automated inventory updates with sales
- Purchase order management
- Low stock alerts
- Supplier management

### 3. Online Ordering Integration
- Integration with 100+ food delivery platforms (Zomato, Swiggy, etc.)
- Single dashboard for all online orders
- Unified menu management across platforms
- Order aggregation and routing

### 4. Reporting & Analytics
- 80+ pre-built reports
- Real-time sales analytics
- Multi-outlet dashboard
- City-wise and zone-wise groupings
- Financial reports and profit analysis

### 5. Additional Features
- Payroll management
- Task management
- Invoice digitization
- GST billing compliance
- Payment gateway integration

## What PetPooja Does Well

PetPooja excels at **operational efficiency** for traditional restaurants:

- Streamlines order-to-kitchen workflow
- Reduces billing time
- Centralizes multi-platform orders
- Provides comprehensive reporting
- Scales across multiple outlets

**Target Market**: Traditional restaurants focused on speed, efficiency, and profit maximization.

## Innovative Features for Sakshi (NOT in PetPooja)

The following features are designed specifically for Sakshi's conscious living mission and are NOT copies of PetPooja:

### 1. **Conscious Dining Experience Tracker**

**Concept**: Track and gamify the holistic dining experience beyond just ordering food.

**Features**:
- **Mindful Eating Timer**: Encourage customers to eat slowly (20+ minutes per meal)
- **Gratitude Journaling**: In-app prompts to reflect on meal experience
- **Dosha Balance Feedback**: Rate how they feel after eating (energized, heavy, balanced)
- **Food Waste Photo Upload**: Customers photograph their plate before leaving
- **Meditation Before Meals**: Optional 2-minute guided meditation at table
- **Seva Token Rewards**: Earn tokens for mindful eating practices

**Why Unique**: PetPooja tracks transactions; Sakshi tracks transformation.

---

### 2. **Community Meal Sponsorship System**

**Concept**: Enable affluent customers to sponsor meals for those in need, creating a transparent giving ecosystem.

**Features**:
- **Sponsor-a-Meal Button**: One-click to sponsor 1, 5, or 10 community-priced meals
- **Real-Time Impact Board**: Digital display showing "15 meals sponsored today"
- **Anonymous Matching**: Sponsors never know recipients, recipients never know sponsors
- **Gratitude Exchange**: Recipients can leave anonymous thank-you notes
- **Monthly Sponsor Leaderboard**: Recognize top sponsors (with permission)
- **Tax Receipt Generation**: Automatic 80G receipts for donations

**Why Unique**: PetPooja handles payments; Sakshi facilitates compassion.

### 3. **Farm-to-Table Transparency Chain**
