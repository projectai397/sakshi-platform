## Understanding Triple Pricing

### The Three Tiers

**üíô Community Tier (40% of fair price)**

This tier is for those who need financial support. We believe everyone deserves access to healthy, nourishing food regardless of their economic situation.

*Example: Sattvic Thali - ‚Çπ150*

**üíö Fair Tier (True cost)**

This tier covers the actual cost of ingredients, preparation, staff, and sustainable operations. Most customers choose this tier.

*Example: Sattvic Thali - ‚Çπ250*

**üß° Supporter Tier (150% of fair price)**

This tier helps subsidize community tier access. By paying a bit more, you're helping others eat healthy too!

*Example: Sattvic Thali - ‚Çπ350*

### Choosing Your Tier

**How to decide:**

1. **Assess your situation** - Be honest about what you can afford
2. **No judgment** - All tiers receive the same quality and service
3. **Pay it forward** - If you can afford supporter tier, you're helping others
4. **Mix and match** - You can choose different tiers for different items

**Privacy guaranteed:**
- Your tier choice is never visible to staff or other customers
- All orders are prepared with equal care and attention
- No questions asked, no verification required

## Placing Orders

### Order Types

**Dine-In**
- Visit our cafe location
- Order at counter or via app
- Enjoy in our mindful eating space

**Delivery**
- Available within 10km of cafe locations
- Delivery fee: ‚Çπ30-50 based on distance
- Estimated time: 30-45 minutes

**Pickup**
- Order ahead via app
- Pick up at scheduled time
- Skip the wait

**Catering**
- For events and gatherings
- Minimum order: 10 people
- Advance booking required (48 hours)

### Placing an Order

1. **Browse menu** and add items to cart
2. **Select pricing tier** for each item
3. **Choose order type** (dine-in/delivery/pickup)
4. **Add delivery address** (if applicable)
5. **Review order** and total
6. **Select payment method**
   - Credit/debit card
   - UPI
   - Seva tokens (if available)
7. **Add special instructions** (optional)
8. **Confirm order**

### Order Tracking

Track your order status in real-time:
- **Pending** - Order received, awaiting confirmation
- **Confirmed** - Order accepted, preparation starting
- **Preparing** - Your food is being made with love
- **Ready** - Ready for pickup or out for delivery
- **Delivered** - Enjoy your meal!

Access order history in **Dashboard ‚Üí My Orders**

- Update meal preferences

- Adjust delivery times

**Cancel Subscription:**
- Cancel anytime, no penalty

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is the menu completely vegan?**
A: Yes! All items are 100% plant-based, made with whole foods and minimal processing.

**Q: Do you use onion and garlic?**
A: No, we follow Satvic principles which exclude onion, garlic, and other tamasic foods.

**Q: Are your ingredients organic?**
A: We source organic ingredients whenever possible and prioritize local, seasonal produce.

**Q: Do you accommodate allergies?**
A: Yes! All menu items list allergens. You can filter by allergen-free options.

### Ordering Questions

**Q: What's the minimum order for delivery?**
A: No minimum order! However, delivery fee applies to all orders.

**Q: Can I schedule orders in advance?**
A: Yes, you can schedule orders up to 7 days in advance.

**Q: What if I'm not satisfied with my order?**
A: Contact us within 24 hours for a full refund or replacement, no questions asked.

**Q: Can I customize menu items?**
A: Yes! Add special instructions during checkout for modifications.

### Pricing Questions

**Q: How do I know which pricing tier to choose?**
A: Choose based on what you can comfortably afford. There's no verification or judgment.

**Q: Will staff know which tier I chose?**
A: No, your tier choice is completely private.

**Q: Can I change tiers between orders?**
A: Yes, you can choose any tier for each order.

**Q: What if I can't afford even the community tier?**
A: Contact us directly - we never turn anyone away due to inability to pay.

### Class Questions

**Q: Do I need cooking experience?**
A: No! We have classes for all levels, from complete beginners to advanced cooks.

**Q: What if I miss a class I registered for?**
A: Virtual classes are recorded. In-person classes can be rescheduled once for free.

**Q: Can I bring a friend to a class?**
A: Each person needs their own registration, but we offer group discounts for 3+ people.

**Q: Are classes suitable for children?**
A: We have dedicated kids' classes. Children 12+ can join adult beginner classes with parent.

### Subscription Questions

**Q: Can I pause my subscription while traveling?**
A: Yes, pause anytime and resume when you're back.

**Q: What if I don't like a meal in my subscription?**
A: You can exclude specific items in your preferences, or contact us for a one-time replacement.

**Q: How far in advance can I skip a delivery?**
A: Skip up to 24 hours before scheduled delivery time.

**Q: Is there a contract or commitment period?**
A: No contract! Cancel anytime with no penalty.

### Health Tracking Questions

**Q: Is my health data private?**
A: Absolutely. Your data is encrypted, never shared, and completely under your control.

**Q: Can I export my health data?**
A: Yes, download your complete data anytime in CSV format.

**Q: Do I have to track my health to use the cafe?**
A: No, health tracking is completely optional.
