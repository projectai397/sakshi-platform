ERC20 utility token turns users into stakeholders and rewards positive community actions

Earning SAK Tokens

Quality product listings and accurate descriptions
Repairing items instead of discarding them
Writing helpful reviews and guides
Referring new community members
Attending events and volunteering

Spending SAK Tokens

Discounts on purchases (up to 50% off)
Access to exclusive or limited items
Premium features and early access
Voting power in community governance
Economic Model: Staking rewards encourage long-term holding, while governance rights create true ownership
