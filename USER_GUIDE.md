## 2. The Sakshi Economy

### Triple Pricing System
Every item on our platform has three prices, ensuring access for everyone:

- **Community Price:** If money is tight, choose this option. No questions asked.
- **Fair Price:** This is the true cost of our operations. Choose this if you can afford it.
- **Supporter Price:** Your generosity helps subsidize the Community Price for others.

### Seva Token Economy
Seva Tokens are our community currency, rewarding consciousness and contribution.

**How to Earn Seva Tokens:**
- **Meditate:** Earn 1 token for every 30 minutes of meditation in the Sakshi app.
- **Volunteer:** Earn 2 tokens per hour for volunteering at our events or caf√©s.

## 3. Shopping on Sakshi

### Finding Products
- **Search:** Use our smart search to find items by keyword, category, or even by uploading a photo (Visual Search).
- **Filters:** Narrow down your search by category, size, condition, and age range (for children's items).

### Product Pages
- **Digital Product Passport (DPP):** View the item's entire history, from its original creation to its journey through our community.
- **Environmental Impact:** See the estimated CO2 and water savings from choosing a pre-loved item.
- **Triple Pricing:** Select the price that works for you.

### Checkout
- **Payment Options:** Pay with a credit card, PayPal, or your Seva Tokens.
- **Request Free:** For essential items, you can request them for free through a simple form.
